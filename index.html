Visitor Info & FAQ

Everything you need to know for your visit to Afemai Wonder City: tickets, hours, and park rules.

What are the park hours?

Monday - Sunday: 9:00 AM - 10:00 PM

How much is admission?

General Admission: ₦3,000 per person. Children under 3 enter free.

Are food & drinks allowed?

Outside food is not allowed. We have restaurants, outdoor bars, and snack stalls inside.

Safety rules?

Follow all ride rules, supervise children, and wear safety equipment where required.
